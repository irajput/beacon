What to do in case of a flood

Be prepared

How can you get prepared?

-Have a disaster kit ready

-Be prepared to evacuate

-Have a disaster plan
-Restrict movements and avoid underground spaces

What to do when there is a flood

-Do not wait until you see rising water

-Get out of low areas and move to high ground

-Do not drive through flooded areas

-Keep clear of power lines and electrical wires

-Avoid flooded areas and do not attempt to cross flooding water

-Abandon your car if it starts to stall
